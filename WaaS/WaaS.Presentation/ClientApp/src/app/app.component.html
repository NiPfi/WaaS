What's WaaS?

WaaS (Webcrawler as a Service) lets you keep track of news on your favourite websites. You can define a text or a RegEx pattern WaaS should look for and we'll notify you by E-Mail as soon as WaaS finds your query on the website. If you've been checking in on your favourite blog, band's website or any other website repeatedly only to find out that the news you've been waiting for hasn't released yet, WaaS is the service for you! Using it, you're notified as soon as you

How does WaaS work?

WaaS uses RegEx (Regular Expressions) to find and match patterns on the target URLs you define. RegEx is very powerful but can be a bit complicated but don't worry! You can also just enter words or a sentence WaaS should look for. If you want to learn more about RegEx, you can check out any RegEx tutorial, that's to your liking.
